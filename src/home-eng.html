About us

“GlobEnergy OÜ” - an innovative company specializing in the purchase, sale and processing of food waste and used vegetable oil.

We support the principles of responsibility, innovation and sustainable development. Mutual benefit, trust and professionalism are the main values ​​of our company.

We work with the highest standards and provide the best solutions for our customers, helping them implement green practices in their processes.

Our work helps reduce the burden on the environment and contributes to the development of a green economy. We believe that every small step can lead to big positive changes, and that is why we strive to implement the concept of "waste is a valuable resource" in our daily activities.

  • Partnership approach
  • Environmental responsibility
  • Reduction of CO2 emissions
  • Stimulation of the green economy
  • Quality control
  • Certification and standards
  • An effective logistics solution

Transport

GlobEnergy OÜ cooperates with many logistics companies to ensure efficient and reliable transportation of its goods and services. This strategic cooperation allows us to provide optimal routes, reduce delivery times and increase customer satisfaction by providing high quality logistics services.
GlobEnergy OÜ can quickly respond to changes in market conditions and provide a flexible approach to the logistics requirements of its customers.
